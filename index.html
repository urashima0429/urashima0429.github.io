Ryota Miyagi
Ph.D. Student in Information Physics and Computing, the University of Tokyo / Software Engineer

Welcome to my personal website! I am a Ph.D. student in Information Physics and Computing at the University of Tokyo. My research interests include reconfigurable computing, hardware/software co-design, parallel computing in AI/ML, and system-level design methodology. I am currently working on the development of a high-performance FPGA-based accelerator for Bayesian network structure learning. I am also a software engineer with experience in developing web applications and mobile applications. I am passionate about technology and enjoy learning new things. After graduating, I am looking to work in the industry rather than academia, and I hope to do so in Japan. Feel free to contact me if you have any questions or would like to collaborate on a project.
